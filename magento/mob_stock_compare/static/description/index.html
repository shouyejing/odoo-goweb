Magento Stock Comparison

Real Time Stock Comparison

Fetch stock from Odoo and Magento as well which are mapped at both ends and compare them on the basis of stock.

Automated Stock Sync

For autmation sync of mis-match prodcuts stcok just make the active MOB Stock Sync Scheduler.

Product stock comparison list view

Using MOB stock comparison module user can find the list of products highlighted in red showing stock mis match between odoo and magento. This extension makes it simpler for the MOB user to find the product stock mis match.

Update or correct the stock mis-match

Once the products having stock mis-match are highlighted on the stock comparison page then you may update or correct the stock mis-match by simply selecting the products showing stock mis-match and then click on the action button to synchronize product stock to magento.
